Disable randomized MAC

Randomized MAC addresses can change over time and make long-term access impossible.

Why it's ok to disable this on our Wi-Fi

On our managed Wi-Fi network, we use the device MAC address to keep you connected to your account. Disabling randomization here does not affect your privacy elsewhere.
